Balltopia

  1. PLAY STORE

Content

Balltopia is a puzzle platformer where you must collect all the green tokens in each level in order to advance to the next one. Different mechanics such as jump pads, moving platforms, and teleporters get slowly introduced, making each level more difficult than the last.

The game features brand new player customization with tonnes of variety to choose from including different ball styles, and also different colours available to the player. With tonnes of combinations of balls to choose from, jump your way through 12 levels, with more levels coming soon!
